Hassan's Top Ten Cars
By: Hassan Sajid
Welcome to Hassan’s “Top Ten Cars” list. Here I, Hassan, will review and show you my personal top ten cars. There is a hidden theme regarding the types of cars I like, so let’s see if you’ll be able to spot it.

Side Note: This list does not contain any supercars.

1. Corvette C5
The C5 Corvette (1997-2004) is known for offering great performance at a good price. It comes with a powerful LS1 V8 engine that produces around 345-350 horsepower, allowing it to go from 0 to 60 mph in about 4.7 seconds. It handles well thanks to its strong, lightweight frame and smoother suspension with a plus of the interior being more comfortable than older models. Overall, the C5 is loved for its mix of speed, handling, and affordability.
