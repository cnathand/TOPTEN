Hassan's Top Ten Cars
By: Hassan Sajid
Welcome to Hassan’s “Top Ten Cars” list. Here I, Hassan, will review and show you my personal top ten cars. There is a hidden theme regarding the types of cars I like, so let’s see if you’ll be able to spot it.

Side Note: This list does not contain any supercars.

1. Corvette C5
The C5 Corvette (1997-2004) is known for offering great performance at a good price. It comes with a powerful LS1 V8 engine that produces around 345-350 horsepower, allowing it to go from 0 to 60 mph in about 4.7 seconds. It handles well thanks to its strong, lightweight frame and smoother suspension with a plus of the interior being more comfortable than older models. Overall, the C5 is loved for its mix of speed, handling, and affordability.

2. Mazda RX-7
The Mazda RX-7 is famous for its lightweight design and unique rotary engine, which gives it a distinct performance feel. The turbocharged 1.3L rotary engine delivers around 252 horsepower in later models, helping it reach 0-60 mph in about 5 seconds. The RX-7 is well-regarded for its sharp handling and balanced weight distribution, making it a favorite for driving enthusiasts. While its rotary engine requires more maintenance and care, the RX-7 stands out for its sleek design, agility, and fun-to-drive nature, especially on twisty roads or tracks.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

3. S13 240sx
The Nissan S13 240SX (1989-1994) is popular for its lightweight build, rear-wheel drive, and great handling, making it a favorite for drifting and car tuning. Its 2.4L engine produces around 140-155 horsepower, which isn’t very fast stock, but it’s fun to drive and easy to upgrade. The S13’s balance, affordable price, and huge aftermarket support make it a top choice for car enthusiasts. Its stylish design and strong driving feel keep it loved in the car scene today.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

4. MK3 Supra
The Toyota MK3 Supra (1986-1992) is known for its solid performance, rear-wheel drive, and turbocharged engine option. With a 3.0L inline-6 engine that produces around 200-230 horsepower, or up to 276 horsepower with the turbo model, it offers strong acceleration and a smooth ride. Its good handling and comfortable interior made it a well-rounded sports car of its time. While it’s heavier than some competitors, the MK3 Supra is still a favorite for its power, modifiability, and classic 80s design.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

5. Honda NSX
The Honda NSX (1990-2005) is known for combining supercar performance with everyday reliability. Its 3.0L or 3.2L V6 engine delivers 270-290 horsepower, offering strong acceleration and excellent handling. With a lightweight design and balanced mid-engine layout, it’s fun to drive both on the road and track. The NSX stands out for being comfortable and easy to use, making it an iconic and reliable sports car.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

6. BMW 850i
The BMW 850i (1990-1999) is a luxury grand tourer with a powerful 5.0L V12 engine that produces around 296 horsepower. Known for its smooth performance and refined design, it offers a comfortable ride with strong acceleration. Its advanced features for the time, like pop-up headlights and a sleek, aerodynamic body, made it stand out. While it's heavier than a typical sports car, the 850i is praised for its high-speed stability, luxury, and classic 90s style.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

7. MK5 Supra
The Toyota MK5 Supra (2020-present) is a modern sports car with great handling and power. It has a 3.0L turbo engine that makes 382 horsepower, going from 0-60 mph in under 4 seconds. Built with help from BMW, it has a comfortable interior and advanced features. The MK5 is loved for its sleek design, fun driving experience, and potential for tuning.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

8. Nissan GT-R
The Nissan GT-R (2007-present) is known for its incredible performance and advanced technology. Powered by a twin-turbo 3.8L V6 engine that produces up to 600 horsepower, it can go from 0-60 mph in under 3 seconds. The GT-R’s all-wheel drive and precise handling make it a beast on both the road and track. With its aggressive design and high-tech features, the GT-R offers supercar performance at a more accessible price, making it a favorite among enthusiasts.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

9. Lexus LC-500
The Lexus LC500 is a luxury grand tourer known for its striking design and smooth performance. Powered by a 5.0L V8 engine that delivers 471 horsepower, it combines strong acceleration with a refined, comfortable ride. The LC500 stands out for its high-quality interior, advanced tech, and a mix of sportiness and luxury. With its sleek look and excellent craftsmanship, it offers a balance of power, comfort, and style, making it a top choice for those seeking both performance and elegance.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

10. FL5 Honda Civic
The Honda Civic Type R FL5 (2023-present) is a fast and fun hatchback with a 315-horsepower turbocharged 2.0L engine. It offers great handling, a 6-speed manual, and sharp steering for an exciting drive. With better aerodynamics and a more comfortable interior, it’s great for both the track and daily use. It’s loved for its mix of speed, performance, and practicality.
