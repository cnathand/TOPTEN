Nate's Top Ten Sodas

Sodas come in a wide range of flavors and styles, from classic colas like Coke and Pepsi to fruity options like Orange Crush and Mountain Dew. Citrus-based sodas like Sprite and 7UP offer a lighter, refreshing taste, while root beers like Mug provide a smooth, creamy experience. There are also unique options like Dr. Pepper, which has a distinctive flavor, and specialty drinks like Baja Blast with its tropical twist. Many sodas now come in zero-sugar versions, offering the same great taste without the sugar. There's a soda for every preference and occasion!
